My Story

Blending Vietnamese heritage with African-American culture through performance arts.

From Saigon to Stages Worldwide

Born in Ho Chi Minh City and raised in Atlanta, my multicultural background fuels my artistic expression. I bring unique perspectives to every role and performance.

Trained in traditional Vietnamese music and contemporary hip-hop dance, my work bridges cultural divides while celebrating our shared humanity.
